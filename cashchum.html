Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is my financial data secure with Cashchum? +

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, and your data is never sold or shared with third parties. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and comply with all major financial regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

How does the AI-powered savings work? +

Our AI analyzes your income, spending patterns, and goals to automatically transfer small amounts to your savings when you can afford it. It learns your habits over time and optimizes savings without impacting your daily spending.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes! There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can downgrade to our free plan or cancel completely at any time from your account settings. Your data remains accessible for 90 days after cancellation.

Which banks are supported? +

Cashchum works with over 10,000 financial institutions across North America, Europe, and Australia. This includes all major banks and most credit unions. You can check if your bank is supported during the signup process.

What happens during the free trial? +

You get full access to all Pro features for 30 days with no credit card required. We'll send you a reminder before the trial ends. If you don't upgrade, you'll automatically continue on our free plan with no interruption.

Does Cashchum charge any hidden fees? +

Never. The price you see is the price you pay. There are no transaction fees, account fees, or hidden charges. Your subscription covers unlimited transactions and all features within your plan tier.
